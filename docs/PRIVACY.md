# Privacy Summary

This is a short public summary for the GitHub information repository. It is not a full legal policy.

Primary landing/legal pages should remain the canonical public legal surface.

## Core model

Memory Palace is local-first.

That means:

- raw documents live on your machine,
- compiled wiki notes live on your machine,
- generated outputs live on your machine,
- the app does not use AlphaTech as a document-hosting backend.

## What may leave the machine

Depending on configuration and workflow, the app may send data to:

- the LLM provider the user configures,
- an STT provider if API-based speech-to-text is chosen,
- websites/YouTube/GitHub when the user fetches remote sources,
- product license/update endpoints.

## What this repo should not claim

Do not claim:

- "zero network traffic",
- "fully offline in every workflow",
- "AlphaTech never receives any metadata at all",
- "all speech-to-text is always local".

Those claims are broader than the actual product boundary.

## Better public wording

Use wording like:

- "Your documents and notes stay on your machine."
- "Memory Palace does not upload your document library to AlphaTech servers."
- "Network use depends on the provider and source workflow you choose."

## Files and ownership

The product outputs standard files such as Markdown and Canvas artifacts. Users retain direct access to those files outside the app.
